About Tech Mahotsav 2025

Tech Mahotsav is India's biggest national-level technology festival that celebrates innovation, creativity, and learning.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower youth through practical exposure to technology, workshops, competitions, and knowledge sharing.

🌟 Our Vision

To become India’s most impactful and inclusive tech fest that connects learners with industry professionals.

📜 Our Story

Started in 2018 by a group of tech enthusiasts, Tech Mahotsav has grown into a community of over 50,000 participants nationwide.
