Tips for Kunwara Bhivsen Trip

Tips for Kunwara Bhivsen Trip

1. Be careful while climbing

The way to the fort is rocky and sometimes slippery. Wear strong shoes and walk in a group.

2. Carry snacks and water

You can find some food there, but it’s better to carry your own snacks and water bottle.

3. Enjoy boating

You can enjoy boating here. The cost is around ₹50 per person.

4. Be alert in forest area

Some people say there may be tigers or crocodiles, but we didn’t see any. Still, be alert and follow safety rules.

5. Keep the place clean

Do not throw garbage. Keep the place clean and safe for everyone.
